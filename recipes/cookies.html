    The best chocolate chip cookie recipe ever

    Delicious homemade cookies

    Ingredients:

    • Chocolate chips
    • Butter
    • Sugar
    • Eggs
    • Vanilla
    • Flour

    Steps:

    1. The first step in making these easy chocolate chip cookies to to combine the dry ingredients in a medium size bowl.
    2. Next, cream together butter and sugars
    3. Add dry ingredients and stir until just combined
    4. At this point you can either bake your dough or freeze it.
